What is Nfold?

Ribonucleic acid or RNA, is one of the three major macromolecules (along with DNA and proteins) essential for all known forms of life. Like DNA, RNA is made up of a long chain of components called nucleotides.The sequence of nucleotides allows RNA to encode genetic information.Some RNA molecules play an active role in cells by catalyzing biological reactions, controlling gene expression, or sensing and communicating responses to cellular signals.One of these active processes is protein synthesis, a universal function

Nfold is a webserver that is developed with the intention to Predict and Visualize the structures of these RNA. It is developed using Python and Django as a web framework. One can get the approximate secondary structure using this web server. It is based on a slight variant of Nussinov's Algorithm for RNA Folding.
